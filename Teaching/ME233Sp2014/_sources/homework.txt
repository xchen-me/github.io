Homework
========

Policies
--------

* Late homeworks will not be accepted.

* Group discussion is encouraged. However, each student must write up his/her homework on an individual basis. Indicate, at the beginning of your homework, with whom (if anyone) you have discussed the homework problems. 

.. Team work on the class assignments is strictly encouraged, as far as generation of ideas is concerned. However, you have to write your own answers, comments, and your own code.

.. List of homeworks
.. -----------------

* Homeworks are distributed in bcourses.berkeley.edu
