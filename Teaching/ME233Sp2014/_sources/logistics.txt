*********
Logistics
*********

Prerequisites
=============

ME 232 (`syllabus for ME232 in 2012 <_static/ME232_syllabus_F12.pdf>`_) or its equivalence

Grading
=======

40 % from 2 midterms (open one page summary sheet for each test), 40 % from final (open notes) and 20 % from assigned homework.

Linear Quadratic Optimal Control

#. Bryson and Ho, Applied Optimal Control: Optimization, Estimation, and Control, Wiley

#. Grewal and Andrews, Kalman Filter, Theory and Practice, Prentice Hall
